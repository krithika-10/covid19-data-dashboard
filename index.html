🧠Dashboard Insights — Overall Analysis
🌍
The pandemic affected 231 countries, recording a staggering 704.8 Million confirmed cases globally by June 2024. Europe led with 253M cases, followed closely by Asia at 221M.
⚠️
USA topped the world with 111.8M cases and 1.22M deaths — the highest of any single country. Yemen recorded the world's highest fatality rate at 18.07%.
💊
Global recovery stands at 675.6M — a 95.9% recovery rate, reflecting the effectiveness of global vaccination and medical response. S-Korea led recovery at 99.9%.
📈
The overall global case fatality rate is 0.99%, down from early pandemic peaks. Africa had the lowest testing coverage, making its 258K deaths likely an undercount.
🔬
Over 7 Billion tests were conducted globally. Europe and North America dominated testing, enabling better case detection. Countries with higher testing showed more accurate death and recovery ratios.
